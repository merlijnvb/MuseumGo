"How might the fusion of Pokémon Go’s augmented reality mechanics with the curated educational environment of museums impact user engagement, learning experiences, and the preservation of cultural heritage?"

With our services we enable you to:
-- better monitor your visitors
-- manipulate visitors recommended walking path
-- keep a better eye on your room climates
-- attract more visitors with special events and features that our platform offers

We have created a platform that will monitor visitor behaviour throughout a museum or art gallery. It does so by a implementing an Indoor Positioning System (IPS) in combination with high sensitivity climate surveillance modules.

Click the button below to check out our first prototype! For now we simulate only random visitor movements and base sensor values upon 1 arduino CO2 sensor.

What visitors can expect while using our application:
-- smoother overall experience (walk through the less crowded rooms to have the best experience)
-- make art interactive while touring your museum
-- collecting points and unlocking achievements while appreciating art in other ways previously not possible

To ensure you these features and experiences when going to your favorite museum or art gallery we have developed 3 games. These games are:
-- hunting game
-- puzzle game
-- trivia game
